Historical Geocoding

Historical geocoding: the tools

We propose tools for historical geocoding, prepared with data for Paris city between 1800 and now.

If you want to add geocoding capabilities for other cities/time period, contact us, it's very easy to do.


Important :

  • The edit you do are saved and shared!
  • All your results are stored in the server and associated to a Random Unique IDentifier (RUID).
  • Keep your RUID somewhere if you want to access your results from anywhere and anytime
